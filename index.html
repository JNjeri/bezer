About Us

Bezer Grief Support and Counseling Center provides specialist bereavement ,support and counseling services to people, who experience problems in the normal process of coping with bereavement.

 

Bezer exists to promote the wellbeing of bereaved people, to help them understand their grief and cope with their loss. Our mission is to provide a quality support counseling service for adults and children over the age of 6 years ,schooling or working through a dedicated team of specialists, volunteers, interns and staff.

Our Services

Individual Support and Counseling

Different people greive in different ways. At Bezer we have the a team of professionals that provide 1:1 support to cater for individual needs

Support Groups & Seminars

Bezer provides seminar and counseling on grief-related topics, such as coping with loss, spirituality, grief in the workplace, and issues of special concern to the community

Telephone and Email Support

We periodically mail educational materials and updates to family and friends about upcoming seminars, support groups, drop-in groups, and memorial services.
